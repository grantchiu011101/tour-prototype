Creator Audio Recording States

Idle
Ready to record. Mic button is active, timer at 00:00, other controls disabled.
Recording
Audio is being captured. Timer runs, waveform animates, pause and stop enabled.
Paused
Recording is paused. Timer stopped, resume and stop enabled.
Preview
Listen to the recorded audio. Play, delete, or save the recording.
Saving
Audio is being saved or processed. Controls are disabled.
Error
An error occurred. User can retry recording.
