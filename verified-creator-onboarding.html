Verified Creator Onboarding

Follow these steps to become a Verified Creator and unlock new opportunities on Roam.

1. Meet Eligibility
Professional guides, historians, museum staff, or passionate storytellers with relevant experience.
2. Complete Your Profile
Sign up and fill out your profile with a photo, bio, and contact info.
3. Submit Application
Tell us about your background, expertise, and motivation. Include links to your work or social profiles.
4. Upload a Sample Tour
Create and upload at least one sample tour or audio guide that meets our quality standards.
5. Content Review
Our team reviews your submission for quality, accuracy, and engagement. We may reach out for a short interview.
6. Accept Terms
Agree to Roam's creator terms, community guidelines, and code of conduct.
7. Get Verified!
Once approved, you'll receive your badge and access to all creator features.
