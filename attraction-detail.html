Eiffel Tower

Paris, France
The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is one of the most recognizable structures in the world and a global cultural icon of France.

About this Attraction

Standing at 324 meters tall, the Eiffel Tower was constructed from 1887 to 1889 as the entrance to the 1889 World's Fair. Today, it is visited by millions of people each year and offers breathtaking views of Paris.
